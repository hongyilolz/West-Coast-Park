Overview


Huh? Why not go East Coast Park?

Situated in the (farrrr) West side of Singapore, West Coast Park is often overshadowedby its twin - East Coast Park - when families or groups of friends decide on a place to go for an outing. Despite so, there are actually many things you can do at West Coast Park while you get to avoid the huge weekend crowds at East Coast Park!

Apart from the scenic greenery and shorelines, West Coast Park offers many activities such as playing at the playgrounds, cycling, kite-flying, and even barbequing. Read on to find out more!

Adventure Playground


Multiple Play Areas

Comprising of six different play areas, the Adventure Playground contains many exciting facilities to cater to children of all ranges. Apart from the iconic Pyramid, the Adventure Playground also include a Wooden Obstacle Course, Inclusive Playground, Toddler Play Area, Viking Ship Playground and Zip Line Play Zone.

Flying Fox

Wait. Did you just mention a Zip Line?
In fact, we have not one, but four Zip Lines for Flying Fox! Families with children can definitely have fun here!

Cycling


Cycling down a scenic path

Rent a bicycle from the bike rental shop GoCycling for just $8/hr and tour around West Coast Park along the extensive cycling path! Enjoy the evening glow while cycling across the shoreline and you might even find an Instagram worthy scene.

The park connector would also lead you to the nearby Jurong Lake Gardens and Clementi Woods Park.
